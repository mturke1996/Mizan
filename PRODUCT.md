# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Arabic-speaking individuals, small-business owners, freelancers, and daily-wage workers who manage personal money and project work from a phone. They need fast, trustworthy answers about cash, obligations, project profitability, wages, withdrawals, and what action to take next.

## Product Purpose

Mizan turns real financial activity into a clear operating view: wallets, income, expenses, transfers, projects, workers, daily work, and explainable analytics in one secure RTL-first product. Success means users can record an event quickly, understand their exact position without accounting expertise, and make a confident next decision. Supervisors must be able to operate subscriptions, accounts, payments, and platform activity without weakening workspace isolation.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and professional. The voice is direct and reassuring without sounding institutional, playful, or promotional. The product should make accuracy feel understandable and progress feel earned.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative or flashy fintech, noisy dashboards, casino-like gamification, generic AI SaaS styling, excessive gradients or glass effects, over-rounded components, invented financial certainty, fake data, and motivational claims that the underlying transactions cannot support.

## Design Principles

1. **Truth before celebration.** Every balance, rate, projection, and achievement must be traceable to real data and expose uncertainty when evidence is limited.
2. **One clear next action.** Each screen should make its primary decision obvious while keeping advanced detail progressively available.
3. **Calm under financial pressure.** Errors, debt, low balances, and overdue obligations use precise language and useful recovery actions rather than alarmism.
4. **Arabic-first precision.** RTL layout is native, while money, dates, identifiers, and rates remain readable and unambiguous.
5. **Consistency earns trust.** Authentication, finance, projects, analytics, and supervisor tools use the same interaction vocabulary and state handling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Provide a strong practical accessibility baseline without claiming formal conformance: semantic structure, keyboard operation, visible focus, readable contrast, clear error text, touch-friendly controls, reduced-motion support, and meaning that never depends on color alone. Support small mobile screens first and preserve readable Arabic text and isolated LTR numerals.
